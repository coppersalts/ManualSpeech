Controls

These are some essential controls which may not be obvious to a first time user (in addition to some which may be obvious).

-, =– zoom horizontally
_, +– zoom vertically (warning: kinda broken)
Space– play/stop

Hold Shift while dragging a formant to drag the next formant along with it.
Hold Shift while dragging a time boundary to have all subsequent boundaries move with it.
Click a time boundary to open up a dropdown to add phones.
Click on a consonant to change place of articulation and voicing.

Unimportant note: the vocal chords are tuned to A 220 Hz, so you can listen to music in the background and half the time it'll sound nice with it.
